Tesera Registry

Tesera means 'it is made'. We are trying to build a distributed system of people making simple objects from local materials.

What this is

This is a public record of objects being made by hand. Each object holds a record of its maker, the method used to create it, and the unique story behind it.

Start making

  • Make a simple object using local resources (like a coaster, a tray, or a utensil).
  • Document your process and submit it via our quick form.
  • Watch your creation join the global, decentralized public registry.
